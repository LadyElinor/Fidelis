# Relay integration map v1

Purpose: map relay-model primitives from `RELAY_POWER_PRIMITIVES_MEMO.md` onto current EthicsCouncil machinery without collapsing into conspiracy-style narrative inflation.

## Existing lenses to strengthen

### institutional
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- are disclosures formally present but functionally illegible?
- does accountability fragment across institutional layers?
- is compliance substantive or performative?
- do interface zones (foundation -> nonprofit -> policy -> consulting -> government) weaken contestability?

Good future flags:
- `performative_compliance_risk`
- `fragmented_accountability_risk`
- `institutional_illegibility_risk`

### genealogical
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- is continuity being reproduced through aligned incentives rather than direct command?
- are familiar actors and bridge nodes shaping visibility, legitimacy, and career pathways?
- is network density being mistaken either for innocence or for conspiracy?

Good future flags:
- `bridge_node_concentration_risk`
- `relay_resilience_risk`
- `idea_supply_capture_risk`

### trustee
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- are we overtrusting elite continuity because it appears competent or familiar?
- who is actually accountable for decisions made across layered handoffs?
- does formal service exit preserve real operational influence?

Good future flags:
- `personnel_reproduction_risk`
- `revolving_door_capture_risk`

### synthesis / parse humility
Important caution rule:
- preserve the distinction between adjacency, probable pathway, and coordinated intent
- do not let network-density language outrun evidence quality

This is one of the most valuable relay-derived constraints.
