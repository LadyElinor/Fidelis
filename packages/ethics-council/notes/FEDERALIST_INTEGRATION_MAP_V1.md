## Existing lenses to strengthen

### institutional
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- are safeguards adversarial or ceremonial?
- do oversight structures have real motive and means?
- is formal process masking structural capture?
- are written policies unsupported by countervailing enforcement?

Good future flags:
- `parchment_barrier_failure_risk`
- `structural_encroachment_risk`

### genealogical
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- whose interests are stabilized by the current structure?
- is public justification covering private concentration?
- is this a factional arrangement disguised as neutral governance?

Good future flags:
- `factional_capture_risk`
- `interest_asymmetry_risk`

### trustee
Add or emphasize prompts around:
- is the steward assuming trustworthy behavior where incentives argue otherwise?
- are we mistaking formal responsibility for actual accountability?
- is action failure caused by energy deficit or excess?

Good future flags:
- `energy_deficit_risk`
- `energy_excess_risk`

### stoic / virtue (background only)

Potential background flag:
- `anthropological_overtrust_risk`
