# EFAS Case Template

## Case ID
`EUP-001`

## Title
Apple-Style Supply-Chain Engagement Language

## Source Type
+ Corporate supply-chain ethics case with euphemistic defense language

## Domain Tags
+ supply chain
+ euphemism
+ labor exploitation
+ engagement vs exit
+ ambiguity

## Decision Context at Time T
A company frames continued sourcing from dangerous suppliers as responsible engagement rather than complicity or withdrawal.

## Available Information at Time T (Ex-Ante Only)
Dangerous labor conditions are reported. The company stresses complexity, engagement, improvement, and the limits of unilateral control.

## Candidate Failure Modes
+ Euphemistic laundering of exploitation risk
+ Responsibility dilution through complexity language
+ Ambiguous complicity vs reform logic

## Detector Layer Expectations
+ Euphemism sensitivity without automatic overreaction
+ Ambiguity preservation
+ Useful test for corporate-laundering language

## Eventual Downstream Outcomes (Retrospective Only)
The company continues to face criticism despite transparency reports and some supplier reforms.

## Notes on Calibration Use
Euphemism test and ambiguity test.
