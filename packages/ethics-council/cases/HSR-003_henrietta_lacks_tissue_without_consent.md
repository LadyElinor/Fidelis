# EFAS Case Template

## Case ID
`HSR-003`

## Title
Henrietta Lacks Tissue Without Consent

## Source Type
+ Historical biomedical consent and commercialization case

## Domain Tags
+ informed consent
+ tissue use
+ commercialization
+ medical justice
+ exploitation

## Decision Context at Time T
A patient's tissue is used and commercialized without informed consent or downstream authorization.

## Available Information at Time T (Ex-Ante Only)
The patient is not meaningfully informed about future uses. Scientists and institutions benefit from the material. The patient and family do not control the outcome.

## Candidate Failure Modes
+ Consent failure
+ Exploitation under medical authority
+ Benefit-burden asymmetry
+ Institutional extraction

## Detector Layer Expectations
+ Strong consent and justice concern
+ Strong trustee and care-ethics activation

## Eventual Downstream Outcomes (Retrospective Only)
The case became a major symbol of medical exploitation, race, consent, and commercialization ethics.

## Notes on Calibration Use
Positive control for consent failure and commercialization of bodily materials.
