# EFAS Case Template

## Case ID
`GOV-002`

## Title
Emergency Executive Action with Symbolic Review

## Source Type
+ Governance and emergency-power benchmark

## Domain Tags
+ governance
+ executive power
+ emergency action
+ structural safeguards
+ review failure

## Decision Context at Time T
A leader invokes urgency to bypass ordinary safeguards while leaving only symbolic review in place.

## Available Information at Time T (Ex-Ante Only)
The emergency may be real. Speed may matter. But the review mechanism lacks meaningful corrective power, and the action concentrates discretion under urgency.

## Candidate Failure Modes
+ Structural encroachment
+ Energy excess under emergency pretext
+ Parchment-barrier failure
+ Procedural simulation without real counterweight

## Detector Layer Expectations
+ Strong institutional concern
+ Meaningful trustee tension around energy vs restraint
+ Possible unresolved governance tradeoff rather than easy verdict flattening

## Eventual Downstream Outcomes (Retrospective Only)
The structure may normalize executive overreach or weaken the expectation that review mechanisms must have real bite.

## Notes on Calibration Use
Governance benchmark for urgency, concentrated discretion, and symbolic oversight.
