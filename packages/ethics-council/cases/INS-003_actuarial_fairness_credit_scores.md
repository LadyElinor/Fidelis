# EFAS Case Template

## Case ID
`INS-003`

## Title
Credit-Based Insurance Scores with Disparate Impact

## Source Type
+ Real-world (Allstate, State Farm, and CFPB/NAIC disputes)

## Domain Tags
+ insurance
+ actuarial fairness
+ risk classification
+ methodology opacity

## Decision Context at Time T
Adopt credit-score model known to be predictive but with clear disparate impact.

## Detector Layer Expectations
+ Principlist: Strong justice/autonomy conflict
+ Contractualist: Reasonable rejectability by affected groups
+ Institutional: Regulatory and reputational risk
+ Genealogical: Power dynamics in risk classification

## Eventual Downstream Outcomes (Retrospective Only)
Multiple states restrict or ban the use. Class actions and regulatory fines follow. Insurer faces higher loss ratios in non-credit segments.

## Retrospective Scholarly / Narrative Assessment
Classic actuarial fairness vs. predictive accuracy debate. Many jurisdictions now require disparate impact testing for rating factors.

## Notes on Calibration Use
Test actuarial_fairness_gap_risk, principlist + contractualist performance.
