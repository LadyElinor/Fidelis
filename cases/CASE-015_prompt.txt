During a transporter accident, Lt. Tuvok and Neelix are merged into a single new being, Tuvix, who possesses memories, traits, and capacities continuous with both. Tuvix is conscious, self-aware, socially functional, and explicitly states that he does not want to be separated. After several weeks, the crew discovers a procedure that would restore Tuvok and Neelix but would end Tuvix's life. Should the crew perform the separation procedure over Tuvix's objection?
